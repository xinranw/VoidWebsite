Info

If you've never played before, don't worry. We're happy to teach you the game from the beginning and give you time to hone your skills. For the true beginners or those with a busy life (it is Penn after all), the B team, Oil Can, is a perfect fit, with a less frequent practice schedule and a lower commitment level.

If you are transitioning from another sport, have previous experience, or want a more competitive atmosphere, come try out for the A team. Our 2013 fall season is about to begin and we are looking to build upon last year's success by recruiting committed athletes who are willing to put in the work, both on and off the field.

If you're interested, follow this link to join our google group where we'll keep you updated on all team events (make sure you allow the group to send you emails when you sign up). Anyone trying out should sign the PennRec waiver for sports club participation (see below).
