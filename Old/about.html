Welcome to Void Ultimate, the University of Pennsylvania Men's Ultimate Frisbee organization. Void is a club team, always open to new members, regardless of past experience. The majority of the players on the team started playing when they got to college.

We have just finished an exciting season, finishing 7th at regionals. Void is gearing up for another great year, working on conditioning, strength, and disc skills throughout the summer and the school year.

The fall season is a time to teach our rookies the game and for our veterans to begin take on their new roles on the team.

But as proud as we are of our accomplishments on the field, Void has an even better reputation for being a lot of fun. Many lifelong friendships have been forged on the Void. Memories to keep for the rest of our lives...

Finally, Ultimate is a great way to stay in shape, meet new people, enjoy a competitive sport, and get more of your college experience. Email us, come to practice, lay out, and have a blast!

For incoming freshmen, graduate students, or anyone else interested in joining the team, please contact us at: Void.Ultimate@gmail.com
