2012-2013 Practice Information

Practice Schedule

First practice of the year will be on Monday Sep. 10th from 5-7pm on Hill Field. Field practices will normally be on Tuesdays and Thursdays from 8pm to 10pm at Penn Park.
Throwing practices will be on Friday afternoons on Highrise Fields.
Please join our Google Group for up to date information.

Field Sites

Directions to Franklin Field: 33rd Street between Spruce and Walnut, by the Tennis courts.

Directions to Penn Park: From Franklin Field, go up the ramp/stairs (Smith Walk) between the green and the stadium... Keep going until you see a bridge that crosses some train tracks. Cross the bridge... then look for Frisbees flying around in the two fields on the left... That's it.
